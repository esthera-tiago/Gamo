What is Gamo?

Gamo is an interactive geography game that challenges you to identify African countries on a map. Explore the continent region by region, from the Mediterranean coast to the southern tip. Each level focuses on a different region, teaching you the shape, location, and name of every African country.

How to Play

  1. Choose a level — Start with North Africa and work your way through all six regions.
  2. Read the prompt — A country name appears on screen. Your job is to find it on the map.
  3. Click the map — Click the correct country. A green highlight means you got it right. Red means try again.
  4. Complete the level — Find every country in the region to finish and earn stars.
  5. Unlock the next region — Each completed level unlocks the next challenge.

Tips and Tricks

  • Start from coastal countries — they are often easier to spot.
  • Use neighboring countries as landmarks to narrow down your search.
  • Pay attention to relative size and shape of each country.
  • Play with sound off or on — the game works either way.
  • Replay levels to improve your score and earn all three stars.
